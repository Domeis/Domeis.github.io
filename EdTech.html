AI Math Education Application Usability Testing in North America

Executive Summary

Background

This study was conducted for a total of 12 weeks for American parents and children to find out the satisfaction and improvement points of the new AI math education app UI and four main functions. Based on the collected data, we validated the changes in satisfaction and usability made in the app UI and function.

Study Design & Procedure

This project consisted of three stages over 12 weeks, which consisted of Pre-FGD, Mid-IDI, and Post-FGD. Participants were assigned a task weekly to verify app use, along with a survey. The weekly survey contained questions regarding app features and questions based on the given task.

Methods

The study was done entirely online due to the geographical limitations. User interviews were done through Zoom and surveys were administerd via SurveyMonkey.
